XPark Jr.

A kids' activity park with props, plants and animals

Plan a perfect day with the young ones at XPark Jr, just off Kite Beach in Umm Suqeim. Located inside 'The Journey', the park offers a sprawling outdoor area that has a variety of different events and attractions for the kids.
Highlights include a vegetable garden, little orchard and a water area for them to splash in. The outdoor play area also includes a play kitchen with props, large wooden jungles and a function area that hosts weekly activities.
