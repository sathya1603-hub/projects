SIGHTS & ATTRACTIONS

Ski Dubai

Play in the snow and go skiiing in Dubai

Try something impossible in a 'desert' city and go skiing in Dubai. Located inside the buzzing Mall of the Emirates, you will find yourself at one the most innovative and exciting tourist attractions in the Middle East when you enter Ski Dubai.
You can easily recognise it from afar – it’s the slanted building perched on top of the mall. Surrounded by a cluster of cosy chalets and restaurants, there’s nothing quite like sliding down slopes as you watch shoppers pass by in the surrounding windows.

Meet the penguins, Ski Dubai offers and more

This is truly one of Dubai's top indoor activities. Play with the Ski Dubai penguins, build a snowman, roll down the hills, ride the Twin Track Bobsled or explore a snow cavern. You can also have a go on the frozen zip-line or catch the classic chairlift to the top of a hill where you can ski all the way to the bottom.
Experience real snow and real winter conditions inside a shopping centre all year round. To unwind after a session here, choose from a plethora of dining options at the mall, from Din Tai Fung to Tribes, or settle down for a blockbuster at VOX Cinemas.
