THEME PARKS

Legoland Dubai Resort

Build a whole day of fun for families and creative minds

Kids are in for treat at Legoland Dubai Resort, which is adjacent to Legoland Water Park. A world of intrigue awaits for families with children aged two through 12 at this amazing theme park from 10am-6pm Mon-Fri and 10am-7pm Sat-Sun, located in Dubai Parks and Resorts.
While here, be sure to go on adventurous rides and rollercoasters and discover building experiences and interactive play across different lands. To add to the exciting outing, book a stay at the Legoland Hotel – a first in the region, the newly-opened property offers 250 rooms that can accommodate a family of five for an immersive Lego-themed holiday.

Things to do at Legoland Dubai Resort

You can build cars and race against friends and family with Lego cars, or take building to another level with robotics at Mindstorms. Looking for adventures? Jump on the thrilling rides in this world, like the Lego Technic Twister which will make you spin with excitement. Or, see the entire park from above on the Kid Power Towers.
There’s also plenty of fun and learning at Lego City, where becoming a fireman or getting a driver’s license is exclusive to kids. Of course, seeing how Lego is made in the factory and a detour to Miniland (where children will feel like giants) is a must.
