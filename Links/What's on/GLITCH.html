GLITCH

An indoor entertainment centre for all ages

An entertainment destination in Deira's Al Ghurair Centre, GLITCH features over 30 different indoor games for all ages spread across a massive area of almost 4,000 sqm. Open every day, this game zone is perfect for those looking for a family-friendly outing.
With bright lights and neon accents throughout the space, kids of all ages will find happy vibes as they make their way through the many zones. Plan your day of fun by purchasing tickets for individual games or opt for a package with which you can try multiple attractions together.

What are the top experiences at GLITCH?

Start off by climbing Newton's Wall where you can defy gravity heading over to Kazu to test your agility with a ninja warrior-style course. Don't miss SkySurf, the city's first roller glider experience where you can 'float' around the venue and take in the view from above.
Other highlights include Loop-de-Loop, where your little ones can encounter plenty of twists and turns before zooming down a 10m-high slide, an eight-lane bowling centre for the entire family and GamePort Arcade, where the young and young at heart can enjoy retro video games like PACMAN as well as new Virtual Reality (VR) experiences. There's so much to do that one visit won't be enough.
