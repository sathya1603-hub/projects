EXPERIENCES

Balloon Adventures Dubai

Rise above the desert with a hot air balloon adventure

Want to see Dubai differently? Choose to float 4,000ft above the Arabian sands on a hot air balloon tour. Balloon Adventures Dubai has created a world first – where passengers get to share the skies (and basket) with falcons, as they experience a one-of-a-kind aerial adventure.
As the longest operating hot air balloon company in the city, with a staff of world-class pilots that have logged more than 60 years of flying experience, you'll be in safe hands as you glide above the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve. The adventure starts at sunrise, followed by a serene hour-long flight that is worth the early wake-up.

A day in the sun with Balloon Adventures Dubai

Depending on whichever way the desert breeze blows, you'll enjoy stunning views of the rolling red sands, rising sun, grazing gazelles and majestic Hajar Mountains. The experience doesn’t end once you land, as you’ll ride in an open-top 1950s vintage Land Rovers to an amazing breakfast, where you can unwind and indulge in authentic treats.
Choose from a variety of packages, ideal for a romantic rendezvous, a corporate celebration or an unforgettable family outing. Those who want to add more of a Bedouin feel to their tour can customise it with a private charter featuring an in-flight falcon show.
