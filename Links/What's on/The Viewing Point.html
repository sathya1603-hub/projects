SIGHTS & ATTRACTIONS

The Viewing Point

See panoramic views of Dubai Creek like never before

Located at Dubai Creek Harbour, The Viewing Point is Dubai's newest observation deck, offering up panoramic views of the magical Dubai Creek, with a stunning skyline backdrop.
The innovative new structure is a 70m walkway projecting 26m over the water and 11.65m above it, offering unobstructed views of both Dubai Creek and Downtown Dubai. You can also witness the city's many different sides as the day progresses - from a peaceful sunrise to the daily bustle of boats going about their business, and into a beautiful nighttime vista after sunset.

Capture Dubai's landmarks from The Viewing Point

Dubai's top attractions are visible from the new vantage point, including the Burj Khalifa and Sheikh Zayed Road highway, as well as newer structures such as Mohammed Bin Rashid library. The uniquely designed bridge is made of painted steel, making it a striking new addition to the city's architectural icons itself.
Whether you're after a romantic moment, a social media snap or a spot of quiet contemplation, The Viewing Point is the perfect place to go.The attraction is free of charge and can be found at the end of a popular pedestrian walkway in Dubai Creek Harbour.
