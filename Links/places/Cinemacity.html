MUSIC & CINEMA

Cinemacity

Watch the latest films in the lap of luxury

Movie fans looking for supreme comfort while viewing the latest releases – from restaurant-quality bites to VIP leather cinema seats – will find Cinemacity in Downtown Dubai ticks all their requirements.
Pre-order Japanese- and Mexican-inspired dishes in the Azul Lounge, before settling into luxurious recliners to enjoy your film in an exclusive, intimate ambience.

Make it a movie night to remember at Cinemacity

The Fountain Views outpost of Cinemacity is the most deluxe, ensuring every screening is memorable. Films are rotated regularly, with the latest flicks scheduled as soon as they are distributed.
Another big draw of this experience is the extensive dining menu, served while you are watching your movie or beforehand, depending on your preference. You'll feel like you're in a restaurant, with options including regular snacks like popcorn alongside fresh salads, sumptuous sushi platters and signature tacos and quesadillas.
